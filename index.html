A-levels

Second year

Channel 8

A-level Graphic DesignNew College Bradford
Producing a comprehensive branding system for a fictional local TV channel, inspired by technical test cards and patterns. I extended the brief to include coded elements incorporating randomisation and graphic generation to blend the worlds of graphics and computing. Later parts of the project focussed on designing branding for local elections news coverage, including set design and 3D rendering. I got an A* for this subject.

My Local Area

A-level Computer ScienceNew College Bradford
A web app which takes a user's postcode and retrieves data from a range of APIs about their neighbourhood, such as weather, local politicians, council areas, nearby GPs and dentists, and local community groups. The project received a grade of A*.
Certain features may be unavailable depending on data providers.
First year

Wayfinding system

A-level Graphic DesignNew College Bradford
Producing a set of wayfinding signs for various locations in Bradford city centre with the aim of making it a nicer place for pedestrians. The arrival of the lockdown meant that the project had to be completed using found imagery without being able to visit the sites in person, allowing me to explore 360° panoramic images. I also designed a family of icons representing major public buildings in Bradford which I incorporated on to the signage.

Skin Kitchen Co branding

A-level Graphic DesignNew College Bradford
Responding to a live brief to design the brand for a local skincare company, I produced a branding system, set of guidelines, and range of application mock-ups. The owner really liked my designs, saying I "followed the brief really well, it would be a pleasure to work with Bradley in future".

Election infographic
